# Equality, Diversity and Inclusion

## My Commitment

Diversity means bringing different perspectives to the table. I am committed to creating a workplace where everyone can contribute fully, regardless of background, disability, or circumstance.

## In My Work

### Accessible Outputs
- All visualisations use clear, interpretable colour schemes
- Interactive plots with hover tooltips for accessibility
- Clear, plain‑language interpretation for non‑technical audiences

### Health Inequalities Focus
- Regional variation analysis (East Midlands vs national)
- Deprivation as a confounder (in UKHSA environmental project)
- Focus on equitable healthcare access

### Workplace Practice
- I ensure meetings are inclusive (rotating chairs, asynchronous options)
- I support reasonable adjustments for colleagues
- I challenge bias when I see it
- I believe diversity strengthens science

## Continuous Learning
- I stay informed about EDI best practices
- I seek feedback on my inclusive practice
- I recognise that this is an ongoing commitment, not a checkbox
